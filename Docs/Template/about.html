About Me

Tech enthusiast, developer, and lifelong learner

Alex Morgan

Full Stack Developer & Tech Writer

Hello! I'm Alex, a passionate full-stack developer with over 8 years of experience building web applications and digital experiences. I specialize in modern JavaScript frameworks, .NET Core, and cloud technologies.

My journey in technology began when I was 12 years old, tinkering with HTML and CSS to create simple websites. That early curiosity evolved into a career where I've had the privilege of working with innovative startups and established enterprises alike, helping them build scalable, user-friendly digital solutions.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy sharing my knowledge through writing technical articles, speaking at local meetups, and mentoring aspiring developers. I believe in the power of technology to solve real-world problems and am constantly exploring new tools and methodologies.

This blog is my platform to share insights, tutorials, and thoughts on the ever-evolving tech landscape. I'm particularly interested in web performance optimization, serverless architectures, and the intersection of AI with traditional development.
